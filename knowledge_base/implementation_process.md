The implementation process should be described as a staged commercial and technical review:

1. Confirm utility bill and tariff assumptions.
2. Validate roof area, site constraints, and grid connection information.
3. Complete site assessment and technical feasibility review.
4. Confirm commercial assumptions and PPA terms.
5. Prepare a detailed proposal and implementation plan.

Specific dates, durations, milestones, and responsibilities are to be confirmed by Aspan Energy.
