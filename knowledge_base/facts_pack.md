Aspan Energy prepares commercial and industrial solar proposals for customers evaluating lower-cost daytime electricity through solar PV and PPA structures.

Approved proposal language must remain conservative until final commercial review. The proposal may describe Aspan Energy as the project counterparty preparing, coordinating, and reviewing a solar PPA opportunity, but it must not invent binding commitments.

Specific warranty periods, contract term lengths, availability guarantees, equipment brands, insurance terms, O&M service levels, financing commitments, and implementation durations must be confirmed by Aspan Energy before customer signature.
